**Approach A: Editorial Noir — Faithful Replication**

- **Design Movement**: Contemporary Editorial / Psychedelic Journalism
- **Core Principles**: Dark authority (near-black nav/footer), editorial white space, bold serif headlines, tabular content organization
- **Color Philosophy**: High contrast black (#111) and white (#fff) as the primary canvas, with a single electric lime-green (#c8f53a) accent for interactive elements and highlights. Peach (#f5d0b5) for membership CTAs, light blue-gray (#e8f0f6) for newsletter blocks — warm and cool accents that evoke the psychedelic spectrum without being garish.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric editorial grid — left sidebar (article list) + dominant right hero image, then full-width horizontal scroll sections for category articles. Two-column split for "Learn" and "Membership" sections.
- **Signature Elements**: Bold all-caps section labels with thin rule lines; horizontal card rows with overflow scroll; announcement bar with emoji sparkle
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Hover states on article cards (subtle underline on title); tab switching for "Top Reads"; smooth scroll
- **Animation**: Minimal — only hover transitions (0.2s ease) and tab indicator slide
- **Typography System**: Display serif (Playfair Display or similar) for article headlines; clean sans-serif (DM Sans or similar) for UI labels and body; monospace for labels

**Approach B: Psychedelic Brutalism**

- **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism with psychedelic color injection
- **Core Principles**: Raw grid exposure, aggressive type sizing, deliberate color clashing, maximum information density
- **Color Philosophy**: Acid yellow (#f5e642) backgrounds with deep purple (#2d0057) text, neon green borders, hot pink accents — a visual assault that mirrors the psychedelic subject matter
- **Layout Paradigm**: Exposed grid lines, stacked blocks with visible borders, no rounded corners, newspaper-style dense columns
- **Signature Elements**: Thick black borders on all containers; oversized category labels that bleed off-screen; inverted color blocks for CTAs
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Jarring hover effects — color inversions, border thickness changes
- **Animation**: Glitch effects on hover, typewriter text reveals
- **Typography System**: Heavy grotesque (Black Han Sans) for headlines; monospace (Courier) for body text
